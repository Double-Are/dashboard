Real-Time

Why keep checking for new content on your Twitter timeline when "Dashboard" can stream you the latest and greatest? Create your own feed using your timeline, mentions or a customized list of friends, news, blogs, ect.

Controller

Want to use your dashboard with an external monitor, or run it from a picture frame using raspberry pi? Wouldn't that require you to get up and manually change your feed? NOPE! I've got you covered. Dashboard can be controlled using a webapp on any smart device.

Weather

Enter your zip code and you'll you'll know whether to walk out of the house with or without your umbrella.

Expand

For those of you that want a bit of extra information, tap "Expand" to access your mentions, trends list and timeline/list.

Widgets/Open Source

I'm making this project open source in case anyone wants to create their own personal widgets, or make the project better as a whole. The project still has a few issues so I'd appreciate feedback, but I hope you enjoy "Dashboard" as much as I do.
